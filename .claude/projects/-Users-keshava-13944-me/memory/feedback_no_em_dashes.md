Do not use em dashes (—) or en dashes (–) in any writing output. Use semicolons, colons, periods, or commas instead.

**Why:** User finds dashes overused and wants proper punctuation.

**How to apply:** Every text output, commit message, doc, code comment, and content file. Replace "X — Y" patterns with "X; Y" or "X: Y" or split into two sentences.
